As a dedicated psychology major at the University of California, Riverside, I am passionate about understanding the intricacies of the human brain and mind. I aim to integrate my foundational knowledge in psychology with specialized training in medicine to contribute meaningfully to the field of brain science and neurological health.

I am committed to a lifelong journey of learning, growth, and service, with the ultimate goal of contributing to advancements in neuroscience and improving patient outcomes in the medical field.
